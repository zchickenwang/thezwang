some miscellaneous thoughts and ideas. hoping someone out there will build them with me



mr. president
personalized "presidential briefing" delivered in the morning via speech / text to facilitate waking up. users choose from functions like the day's schedule, the weather, audio-books, breathing exercises, financial news, or funny stories--whatever helps them start the day off right.

21 21 21
invitation-only gambling club that rents rooms at night where local members can play against different people of their skill / risk level. provides a real underground betting experience in an intimate, less constrained environment.

odd futures
student trading platform for futures and derivatives on trendy / local phenomena, i.e. midterm and final statistics for big classes. ideally using limited, artificial currency so that students may learn market behavior and risk management without the usual monetary costs.

social speaker system
play tracks simultaneously through multiple phones to emulate physical speakers. all phones must share the song, be in perfect sync, and can vary in frequency weights to complement one another.

founder match
platform for discovering project partners / co-founders that is rooted in shared problems rather than skills. twitter-esque feed of genuine problems on which users can discuss and build solutions.

mixtape
o.g. netflix mail-in model using usb sticks instead of dvd's. users add songs they like on their stick to be mailed to unknown recipients. recipients can listen to and modify stick content as they please. backend profiles allow users to rate one another.

mygazine
users choose from all content mediums to create a weekly personal magazine of art, articles, opinions to be shared. a packaged way of curating information that is important to each user.

real garage band
making digital music creation a more democratic and shared experience. essentially a networked version of garage band in which individuals can work on tracks / perform simultaneously.

multiplayer mobile
local party multiplayer app that uses bluetooth / wifi.

chug
a sophisticated yet lighthearted drinking game app for when space / material limitations prohibit all others.
