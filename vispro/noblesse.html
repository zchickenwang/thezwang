NOBLESSE LA

HIGH FASHION STREETWEAR

Fashion label started by Aamir Peeran, Churl Shin, and myself in the summer of 2011. Noblesse started as a creative collective meeting to discuss the burgeneoning streetwear industry, and ended in 2015 with successful couture and prét-à-porter lines. I co-designed the first collection, directing photography and managing finance until fall 2014.
