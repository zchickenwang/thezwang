THE ART CARTEL

BERKELEY'S FIRST STUDENT ART COOPERATIVE

Startup created by my pledge class and myself for SEP, UC Berkeley's entrepreneurship fraternity. We sought to aggregate artistic talent on campus while providing both an online and local marketplace to outlet demand. I designed the logo, flyer, and created the pitch deck.

LOGO ITERATIONS

Core design founded on hand grasping brush to convey artistic prowess. Played around with typeface, spacing, text color.

FINAL LOGO

Finalized design (left) and unnamed alternate (right) for other graphics. Settled on typeface Team Captain in teal with crimson accents.

Flyer graphic we printed on cards to hand out.
