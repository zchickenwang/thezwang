BLACKWATER ATELIER

LOS ANGELES - BASED FASHION LABEL

Clothing line started in summer 2016 by Aamir Peeran and myself after the dissolution of our previous label. Blackwater is rooted in the social and political angst of our time, seeking not to escape confusion but to encapsulate it in elegant line, embodying new concepts of power. As Creative Director I designed the logo and apparel, coded the website, and marketed our first capsule.

LOGO ITERATIONS

Central motif of abstract waves in a cup. Wanted to create a sinous line reminescient of early 20th century art nouveau, balancing between curve and angle, calm and danger, nature and technology.

FINAL LOGO

Final logo with branding (left) and proposed alternative (right). Typeface is Brandon Grotesque.

COLOR CONTROL
BLACKWATER X PANTONE

Proposed collaboration with Pantone color samples. Intended to capture the subliminal power of color variations in affecting and producing emotions. Featuring BWLA color card (above) and various modified collages of existing cards (below).

CON / SERVE BLACK / WATER
COLLECTION PRINTEMPS-ETE 2016

Select design proposals for Spring Summer 2016. Explored various emblems, logo, and text arrangements centered around themes of sumbliminal influence and contemporary class struggles.

FIENDS AND FAMILY SALE

Final selections for Spring Summer 2016 Presale. Featuring Goyard-inspired BWLA print (natural) and CON/SERVE tagline with logo (black). As the first release, we stayed simple with three-round screen printed graphics on SS tees. Focus on brand awareness and promotion, keeping lower-tier pricing for our "Fiends and Family sale" in order to capture a wider market.
